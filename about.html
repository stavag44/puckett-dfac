About This Page

The Ralph Puckett Dining Facility is named in honor of Colonel Ralph Puckett Jr., Medal of Honor recipient and one of the defining figures of the modern Ranger tradition. He commanded the 8th Army Ranger Company during the Korean War and led the defense of Hill 205 in November 1950, an action that became a landmark in Ranger history.

This site is the unit food channel in long form. Technique, pantry, recipes, and whatever else is worth writing down. Run by Stavag.
